Class Hymns

For over eight decades, every Asbury University class has selected a specific hymn to represent their identity. The following is a list of Asbury class hymns, as stated on the official Asbury University website. Since classes, especially today, tend to pick hymns which are well known in the Asbury community, the majority of class hymns are included in this collection. A few, however, did not match the Asbury Hymn Project's selection criteria and are therefore not linked to below.

Text in [brackets] indicates that the Asbury Archives staff have been unable to confirm that information with a primary source.

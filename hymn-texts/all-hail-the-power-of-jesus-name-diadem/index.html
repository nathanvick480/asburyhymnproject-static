All Hail the Power of Jesus' Name (Diadem)

Praise him, all his angels, praise him, all his host!

Psalm 148:2 (RSV)

This hymn text is the only one in the Asbury Hymn Project's collection to be sung to two different tunes. The tune DIADEM is used by the Asbury community far more often than its counterpart, CORONATION. During most of the year, particularly at Reunion, the tune DIADEM is sung. CORONATION is more commonly associated with missions and Great Commission Congress.

Topic

Praise

Meter

Tune

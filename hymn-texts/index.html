Hymn Texts

Have you ever wondered why hymns so often rhyme? Hymn texts (or lyrics) are actually poems which are meant to be sung. Each verse of the hymn is a stanza of the poem, and the poem as a whole follows a specific meter. This means that it's possible to sing any hymn text to any tune which follows the same meter! This makes it much easier for congregations to learn new hymns—they may already know the tune and just need to pair different words with it.

How Great Thou Art

O Lord, our Lord, how majestic is thy name in all the earth!

Psalm 8:9 (RSV)

This hymn is perhaps the "Amazing Grace" of the twentieth century in that it is almost universally recognized and well-known. The words speak of the awesome majesty of the creation which God has made.

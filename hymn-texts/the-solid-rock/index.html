The Solid Rock

"Every one then who hears these words of mine and does them will be like a wise man who built his house upon the rock."

Matthew 7:24 (RSV)

Also known as "My Hope Is Built on Nothing Less," this hymn expresses our confidence on the security and steadfast nature of "Jesus' blood and righteousness." This hymn has gained occasional popularity as a class hymn within the past few decades and was most recently chosen by the Anchored Class of 2017.

Topic

Hope

Meter

8.8.8.8.8.8.8

Tune

SOLID ROCK

Classes

1994, 2006, 2017

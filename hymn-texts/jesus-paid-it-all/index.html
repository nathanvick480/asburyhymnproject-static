Jesus Paid It All

He himself bore our sins in his body on the tree, that we might die to sin and live to righteousness. By his wounds you have been healed.

1 Peter 2:24 (RSV)

The focus of this hymn is on the redeeming work of Christ, who washed away our debt for sin through His death on the cross. Although this hymn was written in the nineteenth century, it has only within the past decade been chosen as a class hymn.
